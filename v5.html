Do you have questions? We have answers!

The most common ones — answered honestly.

How long does the furniture take to dry?

Usually 4–6 hours. Our equipment cleans without over-soaking, so there's no mold risk — most clients sit on their sofa the same evening.

Is it safe for delicate and natural fabrics?

Yes — delicate materials are our specialty. We test every fabric first and use the appropriate method, including low-moisture cleaning.

Can you remove pet urine odor completely?

We remove the odor at the source with deep sanitizing — not just cover it up. Severe cases inside cushions are quoted upfront as a separate line.

Do you clean restaurants and offices?

Yes — evening and overnight cleaning is available for restaurants, offices and stores, so your business never stops.

Will the price change after you see the furniture?

No. The flat price is confirmed before we start and never changes mid-job — regardless of how dirty it turns out to be.

What products do you use?

Safe, eco-friendly and highly effective professional TMF products — pet & kid friendly, residue-free.
