Welcome to Curly Cleaner

When we moved from Ukraine to Colorado, we chose this beautiful state as our new home from day one. We fell in love with everything it has to offer — the majestic mountains, the welcoming spirit of the people, and the vibrant Denver community.

As a husband-and-wife team, we founded Curly Cleaner with a clear mission: to give our neighbors in Denver an upholstery cleaning service that combines professional-grade results with the care only a family business can offer.

Our company is named after our loyal dog, Perseus — the inspiration behind our standards. If a product isn't safe for Perseus, it will never touch your furniture.

Planning to clean your furniture yourself?

We have some important information for you: store-bought cleaners often set stains permanently, over-wet the foam and leave residue that attracts dirt faster. Text us a photo first — we'll honestly tell you if it's a DIY job or needs a pro.

Do you have questions? We have answers!

The most common ones — answered honestly.

How long does the furniture take to dry?

Just 4–6 hours. Our equipment cleans deeply without over-saturation, so most clients use their furniture the same day.

Are your products safe for kids and pets?

100%. We use only professional, eco-friendly, non-toxic solutions — safe for children, pregnant women and pets. If it isn't safe for our dog Perseus, it never touches your furniture.

How is the price calculated?

We quote by phone or from a photo of your furniture. The final price stays the same unless the soiling is critical or the fabric needs special care — and we always warn you about that in advance. Minimum call-out is $99.

Do you work with delicate fabrics?

Yes — that's our specialty. We test every fabric first and choose the right method, including low-moisture cleaning for delicate and natural materials.

Can you remove pet stains and odors?

Yes — we're pet owners ourselves. We remove odor at the source with deep sanitizing, not just cover it up.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

Satisfaction guarantee: if something's not right (within what's possible for the age of the furniture), we come back and fix it free.
